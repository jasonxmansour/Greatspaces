About Us

The Great Spaces team is led by Dawn Willis, a well- respected designer and design project manager in the building/construction industry. Over the course of her nearly 30 year career she has worked on hundreds of projects throughout New England. Dawn’s creativity enables her to work with spaces big and small, modern or traditional, incorporating inspired functionality. She has numerous certifications in the kitchen and bath industry as well as years of experience in the residential and commercial building worlds.

Because she enjoys the challenge of acting as a liaison between the general contractor and homeowner, she facilitates the communication and productivity on the job managing the design portion of the project. Her ability to help minimize potential challenges by addressing concerns in a timely manner is a special talent that helps ease the stress for all parties involved.

Developing a large network of respected industry professionals has granted Dawn access to products & services which provides an advantage to both her client’s budget and to her client's design goals.
